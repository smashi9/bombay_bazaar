Namaste
Morocco

Born at the crossroads of two great culinary cultures, Bombay Bazaar brings the vivid flavours of Mumbai's famous street markets together with the spice trade heritage of the Moroccan medina.

Every dish is crafted with whole spices, fresh herbs, and recipes handed down through generations — cooked with love in our delivery kitchen in Rabat.
